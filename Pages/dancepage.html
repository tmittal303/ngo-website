Helping showcase Talent:

  • In collaboration of Aman welfare society and India’s well- known telecommunication company-B.S.N.L, state level event was organised to encourage and give platform to hidden dance talent among children and women of Madhya Pradesh.
  • Various stages of dance auditions were organised starting from July 2017 to December 2017.
  • We decided to conduct our first level of dance audition in various schools, colleges, clubs and institutions of different cities in Madhya Pradesh. The participants were provided flexibility to choose any of the category between solo dance, dual dance and group dance.
  • The selected participants; both in Indian and western dance forms were then given entry to semi-final round based on their performance in second level.
  • After showcasing their talent in semi-final round, 20 participants were shortlisted and were qualified for the final round.
  • The final round witnessed some outstanding performances and amazing response from audience which encouraged participant’s confidence to do better. The panel of judges decided the winner, 1st and 2nd runner up and awarded them with prizes and BSNL medal. The winners were also given platforms for future.
  • The main aim of our society was to make this event an open category for all age groups to give them all equal opportunity to showcase their talent.
